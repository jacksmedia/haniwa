In the original FFL2/Saga2, the Random number generator would not kick in until after you start a game.  This meant that if you did a hard restart of the game,
the first battle you fought would always be the same.  The outcome would also be the same along with the gold dropped, damage delt, and even the stat/meat/item
gained at the end of the battle. Thanks to code by tehtmi, this is fixed in the hack. Random number generation that is randomized before loading a game with the clock 
in the menu along with loading a savegame.  However, this also means you can no longer abuse the RnG of the original game to gain lots of stats with a character after a
battle by performing the same actions.  If you wish to have the original vanilla behavior back, apply this patch.

SUMMARY: Just to be clear, this patch will revert the new RnG behavior to vanilla behavior, which is not really random at all when you do a hard reset.
