In vanilla FFL2, Robots cannot ever increase their mana no matter what.  This is also the case in A Haniwa's Contingency, but with ONE exception: at
the endgame, you can find a rare item that will increase their Agility, Strength, AND Mana. However, even with this rare item, Robots cannot be as
good as Mutant or Human mages - but they can at least heal and take magic hits a bit better.

This optional patch removes mana from Robots even WITH the new endgame item. This means it will be just like vanilla where there is no possible
way to increase the mana with Robots.
