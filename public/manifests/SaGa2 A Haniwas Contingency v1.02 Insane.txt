Insane: Only pick this skill if you are an expert with FFL2, or if you have beat the hack and feel you are ready to take on a larger challenge.\n
Most of the regular encounters here are similar to Veteran skill, but the mini-bosses and bosses are much harder. You will need to have some very good strategy to win.\n
If you are thinking of doing an oddball party combination (like 4 monsters), consider choosing Veteran instead, unless you are crazy and want to prove something!  :)
