As of version 2.0 of the hack, all characters and NPCs have been replaced with new and/or remade characters.  This patch reverts them all to the default sprites,
although they are slightly "enhanced" variants.
The exception is new monsters and new NPCs. Examples of sprites not reverted by this patch are the Cyborg and new NPCs for children, elderly,
and monsters that have completely different classes (like the scorpion).  Class specific sprites that also didn't exist before
like wizards, commandos, knights, and the samurai boss are also not touched by this patch.
