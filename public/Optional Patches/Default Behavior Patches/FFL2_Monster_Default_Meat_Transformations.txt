By default, this hack lets monsters upgrade much easier/quicker than before. This is because they will rarely ever downgrade, unlike in the original game. For example,
you won't be getting a fly beyond world 1 anymore by eating level 2+ meat.  This patch will revert that behavior back to the default behavior, which means your monsters
can very easily downgrade to weaker variants.  Basically, if you want a greater challenge when it comes to eating meat and upgrading your monster(s), apply this patch.

Apply this patch AFTER you have patched your FFL2 ROM with the hack!
