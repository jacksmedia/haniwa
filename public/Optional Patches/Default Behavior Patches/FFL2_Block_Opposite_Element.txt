In this hack, the Flame shield will block Fire magic, and the Ice Shield will block Ice magic.  However, in the original game, the elements blocked were the opposite
(Flame shield blocked Ice magic, and the Ice Shield blocked Fire magic). This patch reverts to the default behavior of the shields blocking the opposite element.

NOTE: This patch will NOT revert the MAGI behavior. The only way to have MAGI block the opposite element is to apply the "Default_MAGI_Behavior" patch, but this will
also change a lot of other behavior related to MAGI as well. Unfortunately I had to do it this way due to limitations of the game, but I doubt many people will even
notice (especially considering the MAGI immunities were broken entirely in the original game anyway).

Apply this patch AFTER you have patched your FFL2 ROM with the hack!
